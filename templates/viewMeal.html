Bacon and Eggs - Breakfast

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 484

Fat: 38.2g Carbs: 2.2g Protein: 29.7g

Ingredient List

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper

Recipe

  1. heat skillet on med-high heat
  2. crack eggs in bowl; add salt and pepper and scramble.
  3. add eggs and butter to skillet; stir periodically.
  4. once the eggs start to coagulate, lower heat and cover.
  5. place bacon slices on paper towel; microwave 40 seconds.
  6. once eggs are cooked, serve on warm plate.
